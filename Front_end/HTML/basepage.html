Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church

The Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, also known as the Indian Orthodox Church, is an ancient Christian denomination founded by St. Thomas the Apostle in A.D. 52. It follows the West Syriac liturgical tradition and has its headquarters in Kottayam, Kerala, with a global presence among the Indian diaspora.
